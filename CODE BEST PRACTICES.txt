***************************************
Some "Best Practices" for writing code.
***************************************

A few practices that i try and employ for writing code. Certainly not a difinitive list!


  1) Keep it simple. Don't over complicate the code. Just say what needs to be said AND MOVE ON.

  2) Make it clear. Remember, someone else may have to work on what you have written and must be able to easily make sense of it.

  3) Keep a detailed account of any fixes, amendments or additions.

  4) Fix each issue as and when it is discovered. 

  5) Test everything as soon as you have written it and then test again!
